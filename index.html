About me

I have spent 7 years in the finance and mental health industries, where I learned how to address the needs of a wide variety of clientele, effectively communicating with both individuals and business customers daily. I later decided to take my analytical skills and apply them to an accelerated, software engineering program with General Assembly, where I acquired the skills and foundation I needed to become a Full-Stack Software Engineer. This experience gave me the confidence to implement a reliable and clean user interface experience regardless of the client’s requirements.

Since then, I’ve learned Python and Django independently and built a Sudoku application utilizing React and Django. I was contracted at Universum, a local start-up, from November 2019 to August 2020. I contributed to developing a platform using a LAMP stack; PHP, HTML, JavaScript, and jQuery. We developed an operations platform for users to interact with a point-of-sale system across a store or market enterprise.

I am resilient and determined to solve the problem at hand. I have worked in sales & customer service where I have always been focused on providing high caliber support to others. As a Software Engineer, I am driven to help others and solve complex problems in the most efficient way possible.
